Our Events

Events

Farewell

A memorable and emotional celebration where students and teachers come together to cherish shared moments. The farewell encourages gratitude, reflection, cultural performances, and warm bonding, making it a meaningful send-off for students stepping into their next journey.

Picnic

A joyful and refreshing experience that allows students to relax, explore, and bond beyond the classroom. The picnic promotes teamwork, social interaction, outdoor learning, and creates lasting memories through fun activities and shared experiences.

Republic Day

A proud and patriotic celebration that instills national values and unity among students. Through flag hoisting, speeches, parades, and cultural programs, students learn the importance of democracy, discipline, and respect for the nation.
